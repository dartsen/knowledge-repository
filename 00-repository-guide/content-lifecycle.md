# Content Lifecycle

```text
Inbox → Draft → Active → Review Required → Superseded → Archived
```

## Inbox

New or imported content with uncertain placement. The maintainer should classify it during the next maintenance cycle.

## Draft

Content has a clear purpose and location but is incomplete, unverified, or not yet approved as canonical.

## Active

Content is the current primary guidance for its purpose. It has an owner, review date, review cycle, valid links, and appropriate source verification.

## Review Required

Content cannot safely remain authoritative because its accuracy, ownership, placement, scope, or publication status is uncertain.

## Superseded

Content has a named replacement. The archived document must link to the replacement and explain why it changed.

## Archived

Content remains searchable for historical value but is excluded from active indexes and recommendations.

## Review Expectations

- Quarterly: fast-moving security, AI, licensing, and product-limit guidance.
- Semiannual: platform implementation and operations guidance.
- Annual: stable concepts, architecture principles, and business-domain material.
- Event-driven: decisions, audits, historical reports, and templates.
