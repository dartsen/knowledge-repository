# Data Governance Troubleshooting Guide

## Troubleshooting Mindset

When something goes wrong with Data Governance, investigate systematically:

1. What changed recently?
2. What is the expected outcome?
3. What is actually happening?
4. What systems, data, people, or processes are involved?
5. Where is the failure occurring?
6. Who owns the failed component?
7. What evidence do we have?
8. What temporary workaround exists?
9. What permanent fix is needed?
10. How do we prevent recurrence?

## Evidence to Collect

- Logs
- Screenshots
- Error messages
- Run history
- Data samples
- Configuration changes
- Recent releases
- User reports
- Monitoring alerts
- Access records

## Post-Incident Review

- What failed?
- Why did it fail?
- What was the impact?
- How was it detected?
- How was it resolved?
- What should change?
- Who owns the preventive action?
