# Product Thinking Example Scenario

## Scenario

A team needs to apply **Product Thinking** in a practical enterprise setting.

## Context

Product Thinking treats capabilities, platforms, reports, automations, and data assets as reusable products with users, value, ownership, and lifecycle.

## Current State

- Ownership may be unclear.
- Documentation may be incomplete.
- Governance may be informal.
- Troubleshooting may rely on tribal knowledge.
- Success measures may not be defined.

## Target State

- Clear owner
- Clear process
- Clear controls
- Clear support model
- Clear metrics
- Reusable reference documentation

## Example Flow

```text
Intake
  → Context Review
  → Design
  → Validation
  → Governance
  → Use
  → Monitor
  → Improve
```

## Lessons to Capture

- What worked?
- What failed?
- What decision mattered?
- What pattern can be reused?
- What should be updated in the repository?
