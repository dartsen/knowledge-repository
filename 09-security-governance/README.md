# Security and Governance

## Scope

Use this domain for identity, access, secrets, data protection, platform governance, compliance, risk, auditability, change control, AI governance, and financial accountability for technology.

## Recommended Starting Point

Begin with business context, assets, data classification, identities, and risk. Select controls that are proportionate to the workload, then define evidence, monitoring, exception, and review processes.

## Key Concepts

- Identity, authentication, authorization, and least privilege
- Secrets, keys, certificates, and workload identities
- Threat modeling, secure design, and supply-chain controls
- Data classification, retention, audit, and compliance
- Platform, environment, connector, and change governance
- AI risk and human accountability
- Cost allocation, capacity, licensing, and FinOps

## Common Uses

- Review access and identity boundaries.
- Design platform or environment governance.
- Define control evidence and auditability.
- Assess AI or data risk.
- Establish technology cost accountability.

## Learning Sequence

```text
Business risk → Assets and data → Identity and access → Protection
→ Detection and response → Governance and evidence → Continuous review
```
