# Business Domain

## Scope

Use this domain for durable business concepts, capabilities, processes, terminology, metrics, stakeholders, and value models. Project findings and organization-specific implementation evidence belong in Project Delivery.

## Key Areas

- Insurance, policies, claims, underwriting, and customer communication
- Finance, controls, reconciliation, and reporting
- Business operations, service delivery, and workflow design
- Product thinking, outcomes, prioritization, and adoption
- Stakeholder management and decision communication
- Value measurement, benefits, costs, and operational metrics

## Common Uses

- Understand the process before designing a technical solution.
- Define business terms, rules, outcomes, and owners.
- Connect technical metrics to business value.
- Prepare discovery and stakeholder conversations.
