# Templates

Templates are blank or starter assets intended to be copied into a project and completed. They are not examples of an approved solution and do not replace subject guidance.

## Available Uses

- Architecture overviews and decision records
- Current-state and target-state analysis
- Architecture and governance reviews
- Troubleshooting guides and runbooks
- Learning paths and quick references
- Meeting notes and decision preparation

When a completed template becomes durable knowledge, move the completed document to its primary subject or project-delivery location and update its metadata.
