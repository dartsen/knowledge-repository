# Data Architecture Business Context

## Purpose

Data Architecture should always connect back to business value. Architecture work that cannot explain business value risks becoming theoretical.

## Business Value Areas

| Value Area | How It Helps |

| Trusted Reporting | Data Architecture supports this by making decisions, dependencies, ownership, and controls more visible and intentional. |

## Stakeholders

- Executive sponsor
- Business process owner
- Product owner
- Solution architect
- Data owner
- Engineering lead
- Security partner
- Governance / risk partner
- Operations / support owner
- End users or downstream consumers

## Questions Business Stakeholders May Ask

1. Why does this matter?
2. What problem does this solve?
3. What happens if we do nothing?
4. What will this cost?
5. What risk does this reduce?
6. How does this improve speed, quality, or control?
7. Who owns it after delivery?
8. How will we measure success?

## Metrics to Consider

| Metric | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Manual hours reduced | Shows efficiency gain. |
| Defects or incidents reduced | Shows quality and reliability. |
| Time to deliver change | Shows agility. |
| Number of duplicate tools reduced | Shows simplification. |
| Data quality score | Shows trust in information. |
| Support tickets | Shows operational burden. |
| Adoption rate | Shows whether the solution is useful. |
| Governance exceptions | Shows control maturity. |

## Business Explanation Template

> We are using Data Architecture to make sure the solution supports the business outcome, fits the broader technology environment, reduces avoidable risk, and can be governed and supported after go-live.

## Beginner-to-Pro Business Growth

| Level | Business Skill |
|---|---|
| Beginner | Explain why the subject matters. |
| Advanced Beginner | Connect it to a business process. |
| Intermediate Practitioner | Identify value, risk, and stakeholders. |
| Advanced Practitioner | Compare tradeoffs and define metrics. |
| Enterprise Professional | Align with strategy, governance, and operating model. |
| Architect / Strategic Lead | Influence investment, roadmap, and capability decisions. |
