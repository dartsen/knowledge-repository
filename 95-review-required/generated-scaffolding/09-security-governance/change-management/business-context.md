# Change Management Business Context

## Business Purpose

Change Management should connect to measurable business value.

## Value Areas

- Efficiency
- Quality
- Risk reduction
- Cost control
- Better decisions
- Compliance
- Scalability
- Adoption

## Stakeholders

- Business owner
- Technical owner
- End users
- Governance partner
- Support owner

## Metrics

| Metric | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Adoption | Shows whether users value it. |
| Defects | Shows quality. |
| Time saved | Shows efficiency. |
| Incidents | Shows reliability. |
| Control exceptions | Shows risk. |
