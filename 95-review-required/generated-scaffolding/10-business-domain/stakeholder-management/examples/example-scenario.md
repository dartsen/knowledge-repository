# Stakeholder Management Example Scenario

## Scenario

A team needs to apply **Stakeholder Management** in a practical enterprise setting.

## Context

Stakeholder Management is the practice of understanding, aligning, communicating with, and influencing people affected by a decision or initiative.

## Current State

- Ownership may be unclear.
- Documentation may be incomplete.
- Governance may be informal.
- Troubleshooting may rely on tribal knowledge.
- Success measures may not be defined.

## Target State

- Clear owner
- Clear process
- Clear controls
- Clear support model
- Clear metrics
- Reusable reference documentation

## Example Flow

```text
Intake
  → Context Review
  → Design
  → Validation
  → Governance
  → Use
  → Monitor
  → Improve
```
