# Glossary

## Architecture

The structured design of how parts fit together to support a purpose.

## Business Capability

What the business must be able to do, independent of a specific tool or system.

## Current State

How a process, system, or architecture works today.

## Future State

The desired target design or operating model.

## Governance

Decision rights, standards, ownership, approval gates, and controls.

## Lineage

The path that data, logic, or process flow follows from origin to consumption.

## Reference Architecture

A reusable design pattern for a common type of solution.

## Technical Debt

Future cost or risk created by shortcuts, outdated designs, or incomplete work.
